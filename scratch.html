Skiing at Grand Targhee

My favorite ski resort is the resort I grew up skiing at: Grand Targhee. It consistently has fantastic snow, great access to backcountry terrain, and it’s small enough that even though it’s on the other side of Jackson, the lift lines usually aren’t terrible. Some of my favorite memories are from when I was basically two years old and up, skiing there with my family. There’s something special about riding the same lifts and runs year after year and having all those shared stories on the mountain.
