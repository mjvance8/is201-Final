Skiing at Grand Targhee

My favorite ski resort is the resort I grew up skiing at: Grand Targhee. It consistently has fantastic snow, great access to backcountry terrain, and it’s small enough that even though it’s on the other side of Jackson, the lift lines usually aren’t terrible. Some of my favorite memories are from when I was basically two years old and up, skiing there with my family. There’s something special about riding the same lifts and runs year after year and having all those shared stories on the mountain.

This page is just a place to talk about that and, at the same time, show that I can actually write HTML and CSS from scratch for this class.

My Skiing Bucket List

This is an ordered list with unordered lists inside it. These are some of the things I want to do on skis, from small improvements to big trips.

  1. Improve My Technique
    • Stronger carving on steeper runs
    • Get more comfortable skiing switch
    • Be consistent and in control on small jumps
  2. Places I Want to Ski Someday
    • Whistler Blackcomb
    • Jackson Hole (on a deep powder day)
    • The Alps, somewhere with big terrain and long runs
  3. Just-for-Fun Goals
    • Ski 30+ days in one season
    • Night ski during a heavy snowfall
    • Go back to Grand Targhee with my own family someday
